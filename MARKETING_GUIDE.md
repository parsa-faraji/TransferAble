## üìß Email Domain & Setup

### Recommended Domain
- **Primary**: `transferable.app` or `transferable.com`
- **Email**: `hello@transferable.app`
- **Support**: `support@transferable.app`
- **Team**: `team@transferable.app`

### Domain Providers
1. **Namecheap** (~$10-15/year)
   - Easy to use
   - Good support
   - Free privacy protection

2. **Google Domains** (~$12/year)
   - Simple interface
   - Integrated with Google Workspace

3. **Cloudflare** (~$8-10/year)
   - Best prices
   - Free privacy
   - Fast DNS

### Email Setup Options

#### Option 1: Resend (Recommended for App)
- **Cost**: Free tier (3K emails/month)
- **Setup**: Verify domain, add DNS records
- **Use**: Transactional emails (signup, notifications)

#### Option 2: Google Workspace
- **Cost**: $6/user/month
- **Setup**: Verify domain, create email accounts
- **Use**: Team email, customer support

#### Option 3: Cloudflare Email Routing (Free)
- **Cost**: Free
- **Setup**: Add domain to Cloudflare, enable routing
- **Use**: Forward emails to personal Gmail
